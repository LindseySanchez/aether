Why these stories still matter.

Greek mythology is not a collection of quaint fairy tales. It is humanity’s first attempt to understand power, love, fate, hubris, justice, and what it means to be alive in a world full of forces larger than ourselves.

Every modern story you love — from Star Wars to superhero films to the very idea of tragedy — owes a debt to these ancient voices.
